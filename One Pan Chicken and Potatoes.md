## Ingredients

* Chicken breasts
* Baby potatoes
* Olive oil
* Garlic
* Rosemary
* Salt
* Pepper

## Steps

1. Preheat your pan over medium heat.
2. Drizzle olive oil and add chicken breasts.
3. Sear chicken on both sides until golden.
4. Add halved baby potatoes to the pan.
5. Season with garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
6. Cover and cook until chicken is fully cooked and potatoes are tender.
7. Serve hot straight from the pan.
