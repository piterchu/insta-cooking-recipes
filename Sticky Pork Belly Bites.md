### Ingredients

  * 1 kg pork belly, cut into 2cm cubes
  * 3 tbsp soy sauce
  * 2 tbsp honey
  * 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  * 2 cloves garlic, minced
  * 1 tsp ginger, grated
  * 1 tbsp sesame oil
  * 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  * 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  * 2 tbsp brown sugar
  * 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)
  * Sesame seeds (for garnish)

### Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together soy sauce, honey, hoisin sauce, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, rice vinegar, chili flakes (if using), and brown sugar to create the marinade.
  3. Add the pork belly cubes to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes (overnight for best flavor).
  4. Arrange the marinated pork belly cubes on a baking tray lined with parchment paper or foil.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the pork is caramelized and sticky.
  6. For extra stickiness, spoon leftover marinade over the pork in the last 10 minutes of baking.
  7. Remove from the oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
