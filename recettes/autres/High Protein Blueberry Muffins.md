## Ingredients

* 2 bananas
* 250g peanut butter
* 2 eggs
* 100g blueberries

## Steps

1. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
2. Add the peanut butter and mix until combined.
3. Add the eggs and mix well.
4. Fold in the blueberries gently.
5. Pour the mixture into muffin molds.
6. Bake until cooked through.
