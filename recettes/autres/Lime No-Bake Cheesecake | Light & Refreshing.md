## Ingredients

* Crushed biscuits 60g 
  * Melted butter 30g 
  * Cream cheese 150g 
  * Granulated sugar 30g 
  * Fresh lime juice 35g 
  * Hot milk 50g 
  * Gelatin 7g 
  * Whipping cream 150g

## Steps

1. Mix the crushed biscuits with the melted butter to form the base. 
  2. Press the mixture into the bottom of a mold and refrigerate. 
  3. In a bowl, combine cream cheese and sugar until smooth. 
  4. Add fresh lime juice and mix well. 
  5. Dissolve gelatin in the hot milk, then add it to the cream cheese mixture. 
  6. Whip the cream to soft peaks and fold it gently into the mixture. 
  7. Pour the filling over the biscuit base and smooth the top. 
  8. Refrigerate until set, then serve chilled.
