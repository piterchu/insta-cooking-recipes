## Ingredients

* Eggs 
  * Olive oil (regular or lighter pressing for higher smoke point) 
  * Buttered toast (optional) 
  * Seasoned beefsteak tomato (optional) 
  * Mortadella (optional) 
  * Chives (optional)

## Steps

1. Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a pan with high sides until it is very hot. 
  2. Crack the egg gently into the hot oil. 
  3. Tilt and move the pan to baste the egg with the hot oil for 30 seconds. 
  4. Cook until the egg becomes puffy and light, resembling a poached egg but fried. 
  5. Serve immediately, ideally on buttered toast. 
  6. Optionally, add seasoned beefsteak tomato, mortadella, and chives for a complete breakfast or lunch.
