## Ingredients

- 750 ml bottle of bourbon (pour out 1/4 cup or 4 oz; Four Roses bourbon used)
- 3 oz simple syrup (1:1 white sugar and hot water, or try brown sugar syrup or maple)
- 1 oz Angostura bitters

## Steps

1. Pour 1/4 cup (4 oz) out of a 750 ml bottle of bourbon.

2. Add 3 oz of simple syrup directly into the bottle.

3. Add 1 oz of Angostura bitters into the bottle.

4. Seal the bottle and place it in the freezer.

5. Serve straight from the freezer; add a splash of juice, simple syrup, or water if desired.
