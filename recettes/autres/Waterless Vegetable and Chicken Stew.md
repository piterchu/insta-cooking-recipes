## Ingredients

* Chicken pieces 
  * Assorted vegetables 
  * Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

1. Prepare the chicken pieces and clean the vegetables. 
  2. Place vegetables and chicken in a pot without adding water. 
  3. Cover with a tight-fitting lid to retain the natural moisture. 
  4. Cook over low to medium heat until the chicken is tender and vegetables are cooked. 
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your waterless vegetable and chicken stew.
