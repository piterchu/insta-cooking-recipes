## Ingredients

* Shawarma
  * Mascarpone
  * Eggs
  * Sugar
  * Ladyfingers
  * Coffee
  * Cocoa powder

## Steps

1. Prepare a classic tiramisu cream by mixing mascarpone, egg yolks, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Dip the ladyfingers briefly in coffee and layer them in a dish.
  3. Add pieces of shawarma on top of the soaked ladyfingers.
  4. Spread a layer of the mascarpone cream over the shawarma.
  5. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, finishing with the cream.
  6. Dust the top with cocoa powder.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator before serving.
