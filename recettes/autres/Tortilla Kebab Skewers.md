## Ingredients

### Pour les brochettes:
- 3 lbs ground beef
- 2 minced onions
- 1 minced red bell pepper
- 6 garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup parsley
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 2 tbsp paprika
- 2 tbsp coriander
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp black pepper
- 1‚Äì1.5 tbsp kosher salt
- 10 square-cut x-large tortillas

### Pour la sauce:
- 8 oz butter
- 1/2 cup yogurt or labneh
- 1/2 minced onion
- 1/4 cup parsley
- 4 garlic cloves
- 2 tsp paprika
- Salt

## Steps

1. Mix ground beef, 2 minced onions, minced red bell pepper, 6 garlic cloves, 1/4 cup parsley, cumin, paprika, coriander, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
2. Spread the mix on 10 square-cut x-large tortillas.
3. Stack 5 tortillas at a time with one tortilla on top and bottom (2 stacks).
4. Cut into 1-inch wide strips, turn on their side, and press onto skewers.
5. Cut between skewers to form individual skewered stacks.
6. Grill in an oiled cast-iron pan for a few minutes per side, turning 4 times.
7. For the sauce, simmer butter, yogurt or labneh, 1/2 minced onion, 1/4 cup parsley, 4 garlic cloves, paprika, and a pinch of salt.
8. Dip or drizzle sauce over the skewers and serve.
