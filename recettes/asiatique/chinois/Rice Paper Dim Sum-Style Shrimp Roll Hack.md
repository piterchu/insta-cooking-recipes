## Ingredients

**Main ingredients:**
- Velveted raw shrimp
- Green onions
- White pepper
- Rice paper
- Chili oil

**Sweet Soy Sauce Recipe:**
- 2 scallion bulbs (white parts only, pounded to release oils)
- 1 knob ginger, sliced thin (or 1 tsp minced) â€“ optional if served in a bowl, not bottled

- 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce

## Steps

1. Wrap your velveted raw shrimp with green onions and a sprinkle of white pepper in softened rice paper.
2. Steam the wrapped rolls for 10 minutes.
3. Cut the steamed rolls into portions.
4. Drizzle with chili oil and homemade sweet soy sauce.
5. Serve immediately and enjoy.
