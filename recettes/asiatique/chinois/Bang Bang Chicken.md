## Ingredients

### Pour le poulet
* 2 lb chicken thighs
* 2 tbsp soy sauce
* 2 tbsp shaoxing wine
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 2 garlic cloves (minced)
* 1 egg white
* White pepper
* 2 cups Potato starch
* Oil for frying
* Scallion
* Sesame seeds

### Pour la sauce
* 1/2 cup kewpie mayo
* 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
* 2 tbsp sriracha

## Steps

1. Prepare the chicken thighs by cutting into bite-sized pieces.
2. Marinate with soy sauce, shaoxing wine, sesame oil, minced garlic, egg white, and white pepper.
3. Coat the marinated chicken in potato starch.
4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the chicken until golden and cooked through.
5. Remove and drain excess oil.
6. Mix kewpie mayo, sweet chili sauce, and sriracha to make the sauce.
7. Toss the fried chicken in the sauce or serve some pieces without sauce for a non-spicy option.
8. Garnish with chopped scallions and sesame seeds before serving.
