## Ingredients

* 5 eggs
* 1 green onion
* 1 cup chicken bone broth
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp sesame seeds
* 1/2 tsp sesame oil

## Steps

1. In a pot, crack your eggs.
2. Dice your green onions into small pieces and add them into the pot along with salt and chicken bone broth, then whisk well.
3. In a pot on medium heat, cook and remove from heat once you start to see steam escape the lid.
4. Top off with sesame oil and sesame seeds and enjoy!
