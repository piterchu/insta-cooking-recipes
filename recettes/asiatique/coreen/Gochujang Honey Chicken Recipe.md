### Ingredients

  * Chicken thighs
  * 2 tsbp minced garlic
  * 1 tablespoon of ginger
  * 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  * 2 tbsp of gochujang
  * 1/2 salt
  * 1 tbsp sesame oil
