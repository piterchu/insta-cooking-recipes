## Ingredients

* Boiled eggs
* Soy sauce
* Honey
* Spices

## Steps

1. Boil the eggs until they are cooked to your preferred level.
2. Prepare a simple marinade using soy sauce, honey, and spices.
3. Place the boiled eggs into the marinade, ensuring they are fully coated.
4. Allow the eggs to marinate until they absorb the savory, slightly sweet, and spicy flavors.
5. Serve as a quick snack or side dish.
