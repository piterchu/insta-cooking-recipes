## Ingredients

- 500g cucumber
- 10g salt
- 2T Pepper Flakes (gochugaru)
- 1T Sugar
- 1T Fish sauce
- 1T Sesame oil
- 1 1/2T Apple vinegar
- 1/2T Garlic
- 1/2T Pepper Paste (gochujang)
- 1/2T Sesame seed

## Steps

1. Slice the cucumbers.
2. Sprinkle with salt and let sit briefly to draw out moisture.
3. Combine pepper flakes, sugar, fish sauce, sesame oil, apple vinegar, garlic, and pepper paste in a bowl to create the spicy seasoning.
4. Toss the salted cucumbers with the seasoning mixture.
5. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve.
