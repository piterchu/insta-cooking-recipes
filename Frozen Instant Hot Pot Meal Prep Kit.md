## Ingredients

- 40g bean sprouts
- 4 large leaves of napa cabbage (approximately 60g)
- 4 oz thinly sliced chuck steak
- 1/2 block medium firm tofu
- 1/2 cup sliced Korean rice cakes
- 30g enoki mushrooms
- 3 cup beef bone broth
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp black pepper
- salt to taste

## Steps

1. Chop your napa cabbage into chunks and tofu into small cubes.
2. In a small bowl, mix together minced garlic, honey, soy sauce, black pepper, and salt to make your broth sauce.
3. In a meal prep container, add half your chopped cabbage, broth sauce, meat, tofu, Korean rice cakes, bean sprouts, and enoki mushrooms.
4. Seal the container and freeze overnight or until ready to eat.
5. When ready to eat, remove the lid & microwave the container for 15 seconds to make it easy to remove the frozen block of ingredients.
6. In a pot on high heat, add your frozen block of ingredients and beef bone broth and cover with a lid as it comes to a boil.
7. Boil until everything is cooked through and enjoy!
