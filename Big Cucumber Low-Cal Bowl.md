## Ingredients

- 200g Cucumber
- 40g Avocado
- 100g cooked prawns or imitation crab
- 50g Light Philadelphia
- 15g Kewpie mayo
- Splash Light soy sauce
- Pinch of MSG

## Steps

1. Grab a big cucumber.
2. Combine the cucumber, avocado, cooked prawns (or imitation crab), Light Philadelphia, and Kewpie mayo in a bowl.
3. Add a splash of light soy sauce and a pinch of MSG.
4. Mix everything together until well combined.
5. Serve and enjoy your super low-calorie, high-volume dish.
