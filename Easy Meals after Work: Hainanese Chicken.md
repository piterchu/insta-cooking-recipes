### Ingredients

  * Chicken
  * Rice
  * Ginger
  * Garlic
  * Scallions
  * Soy sauce
  * Sesame oil
  * Chicken broth
  * Salt
  * Cucumber
  * Chili sauce

### Steps

  1. Boil the chicken in salted water with ginger and scallions until fully cooked.
  2. Remove the chicken and let it rest; reserve the broth.
  3. Rinse the rice and cook it using the reserved chicken broth with minced garlic and ginger.
  4. Slice the chicken and drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil.
  5. Serve with sliced cucumber, rice, and chili sauce on the side.
