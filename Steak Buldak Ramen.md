### Ingredients

  * 3/4 C Soy Sauce
  * 3 Tbsp Sugar
  * 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  * 1 Tbsp hot sauce
  * 1 Tbsp Rice Vinegar
  * 2 oz Water

### Steps

  1. Boil your eggs for exactly 6 1/2 minutes setting a timer.
  2. Plunge in an ice bath to stop the cooking process.
  3. In a bowl add soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, hot sauce, rice vinegar, and water and stir.
  4. In a jar add in your eggs and pour over your marinade.
  5. Marinate for 2 hours minimum or best overnight.
