## Ingredients

- Shrimp
- Crispy patty
- Microgreens
- Nutty egg salad
- Creamy tartar sauce
- Sandwich bread

## Steps

1. Prepare the shrimp and form into patties.
2. Cook the shrimp patties until crispy (air fryer or pan).
3. Spread nutty egg salad and creamy tartar sauce on sandwich bread.
4. Place the crispy shrimp patty on the bread.
5. Add refreshing microgreens.
6. Close the sandwich and serve immediately.
