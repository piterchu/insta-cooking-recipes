## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs Pork Belly (skin on boneless), poke holes

**Spice mix:**
- 1 Tbs brown sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp five spice

**Salt crust:**
- 1 C kosher salt
- 1 egg white

- 1/2 Tbs white vinegar for brushing
- 1/2 Tbs neutral oil for brushing

## Steps

1. Prepare the pork belly by poking holes in the skin.

2. Mix the spice mix (brown sugar, salt, garlic powder, five spice) and coat the pork belly.

3. Leave in the fridge uncovered for a minimum of 24â€“48 hours.

4. Mix the kosher salt with the egg white to form a salt crust and cover the pork skin.

5. Bake in the oven at 300Â°F for 1.5 hours.

6. Remove the salt crust.

7. Brush the pork belly with white vinegar and then neutral oil.

8. Roast at 450Â°F for 30â€“40 minutes or until the skin is crispy.
