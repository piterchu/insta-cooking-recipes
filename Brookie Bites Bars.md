## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup almond flour
- 2 heaped tbsp peanut butter (@fixandfogg of course)
- 1/4 cup vanilla protein
- 1/4 cup cacao powder
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- Chocolate chips or cacao nibs
- Dark chocolate + 1 tsp coconut oil, melted

## Steps

1. To create the mixture, in a mixing bowl combine almond flour, peanut butter, vanilla protein, maple and coconut oil.
2. Separate the mixture in two halves.
3. In one half add chocolate chips.
4. In the other half add cacao powder.
5. Press sections of each in a silicone loaf tin.
6. Pour melted dark chocolate over the top.
7. Set in freezer for 30 minutes.
