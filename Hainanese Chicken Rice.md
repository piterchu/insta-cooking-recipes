## Ingredients

**Chicken Oil:**
- 200g chicken skin/fat
- pinch of salt
- 4 tbsp water

**Crispy Shallot Oil:**
- 6 shallots
- 7 tbsp chicken oil

**Chicken:**
- 4 tbsp chicken oil
- 2 stalks lemongrass
- 3 slice galangal
- 1 stalk pandan leaf (knotted)
- 2 big scallions chopped
- 30g ginger
- 2.2L water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
- ice bath
- 2 tbsp sesame oil

**Rice:**
- chicken oil
- 2-3 scallions
- 30g ginger
- 1/4 tsp tumeric
- 1 pc lemongrass
- 1 pandan leaf (knotted)
- 2 cups Jasmine rice (wash and drained)
- 2 cups chicken stocks

**Ginger Scallion Sauce:**
- 30g ginger
- 3 scallions white part
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp chicken powder
- 4 tbsp of chicken sauce

**Chili Sauce:**
- 37g ginger
- 25g garlic
- 25g red onions
- 2pcs red chili
- 37g calamansi juice
- 25g water
- 37ml white rice vinegar
- 1.5 tbsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp fish Oil

**Soy Sauce Paste:**
- 6 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tbsp dark soy
- 3 tbsp chicken broth
- 45g yellow rock sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp sesame oil

## Steps

1. Prepare chicken oil by rendering chicken skin/fat with salt and water until golden, then strain.
2. Make crispy shallot oil by frying shallots in chicken oil until crisp, then set aside.
3. In a pot, heat chicken oil and add lemongrass, galangal, pandan, scallions, and ginger. Add water, salt, white pepper, and turmeric powder. Poach chicken until cooked, then submerge in ice bath and rub with sesame oil.
4. For the rice, saut√© scallions, ginger, turmeric, lemongrass, and pandan in chicken oil, add rinsed Jasmine rice, then cook with chicken stock until fluffy.
5. Prepare ginger scallion sauce by mixing minced ginger and scallion with salt, chicken powder, and hot chicken sauce.
6. Blend chili sauce ingredients until smooth.
7. Simmer soy sauce paste ingredients until sugar dissolves.
8. Serve chicken chopped with fragrant rice, and offer the three sauces on the side.
